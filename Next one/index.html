Something about Bots

What is Voicebot?

Voicebot is an artificial intelligence and natural language understanding (NLU) based voice channel for communication that works by converting audio to text format. The AI technology helps to identify the key markers in the speech and conclude the best response to a dialogue. The text to speech (TTS) engine then converts the response into audio or voice to complete the interaction.

What is Chatbot?

Chatbot is a mode of communication that makes use of text messages. Similar to a voicebot, the chatbot also is an artificial intelligence software that can necessarily stimulate a chat or a conversation with the users in the natural human language. The conversational AI also ensures that the experience that chatbot gives is near human.
