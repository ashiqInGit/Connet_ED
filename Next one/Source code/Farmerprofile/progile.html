About Me

Hai everyone.I am a farmer. Farmer’s role is a very important role in each and everyone’s life. I am responsible for the food on your table every day.

My house is in a village.I have a small farm on my own.I wake up early in the morning and go to work.My tractor helps me to plow the land.I prepare the land for planting the crops. Then I line up along with my workers to put the seeds for the crops.I grow rice and vegetables on my farm.

Then, I do harvesting at the right time.I also care by spraying the necessary fertilizers to control insects.Mainly, I depend on monsoons for growing the crops.Even though agricultural machinery has come, some works are done physically. Farming work is done on all days and in all weather conditions.I irrigate the farm with pipes and pumps.Then, I take care of all my farm animals also.Then, I take care of all my farm animals also.To say, I love my farm very much. Thank you.
